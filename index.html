Why a sauna hat?

Your head heats up first — protect it

It sounds backwards, but wool keeps you cooler in the sauna. Here's the science.

🌡️

Heat rises

The air at head level is the hottest in the room — often 20°C+ hotter than at bench level. Your head overheats before your body is done.

🐑

Wool insulates

Thick felted wool slows heat transfer to your scalp, keeping your head comfortable while your body sweats it out.

⏱️

Longer sessions

A cooler head means less dizziness and fatigue — so you stay in longer and get more from every round.

💇

Protects your hair

Dry sauna heat is brutal on hair. A hat shields it from heat damage, session after session.
